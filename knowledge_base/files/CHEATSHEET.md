### **WHERE TO FIND THE ADVANCED DATA (AK, DK, UL, ASV)**

Look at the **very first image** you uploaded (Traditional chart with the Vimshottari Dasha).

1. **ASV (Ashtakvarga Points):** Look at the bottom right table labeled **"Ashtakvarga Table"**. Look at the bottom row labeled **"Total"**. These are the ASV points for each house. (e.g., in your chart, Sign 10 has 39 points. Sign 12 has 23 points).  
2. **AK (Atmakaraka \- Soul) & DK (Darakaraka \- Spouse):** Look at the **"Planetary Positions"** table. You determine these purely by the "Latitude" (Degree/Longitude). Ignore the signs.  
   * **AK:** The planet with the *highest* degree (excluding Rahu/Ketu). In your chart, **Venus** is at 21°30'. Venus is your AK.  
   * **DK:** The planet with the *lowest* degree. In your chart, the **Moon** is at 03°32'. The Moon is your DK.  
3. **UL (Upapada Lagna \- Marriage Blueprint):** This is not printed on basic charts; your software must calculate it.  
   * *Formula:* Find the 12th house of D-1 \-\> Find where its Lord sits \-\> Count the number of houses from the 12th house to that Lord \-\> Count that exact same number of houses forward from the Lord. That sign is the UL. (In your chart, D-1 12th is Taurus. Lord is Venus. Venus is 11 houses away in Pisces. 11 houses from Pisces is Capricorn. **Your UL is Capricorn**).

**GENDER DIFFERENCE FOR AK, DK, UL, AND ASV:**

There is **ZERO** difference in how these are calculated. A male and female DK are found the exact same way. The *only* difference is that in a male chart, Venus acts as an *additional* natural DK, and in a female chart, Jupiter acts as an *additional* natural DK.

**The Formula for Counting Relationships:**

You must look *only* at the **D-1 Chart**.

1. Look at the 5th House.  
2. Count the number of planets sitting *inside* the 5th House.  
3. Count the number of planets looking directly at the 5th House from the 11th House.
