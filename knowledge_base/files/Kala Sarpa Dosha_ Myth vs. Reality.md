Tbh, yes. The concept of "Kala Sarpa" (The Serpent of Time) started appearing much later, mostly popularized in the 19th and 20th centuries.

* **The Trap:** Imagine Rahu (the head of the snake) and Ketu (the tail) drawing a literal boundary line across your birth chart. If all planets are trapped in one half, the other half of the chart is completely empty.  
* **The Reality:** In Jyotish physics, this is not a "curse." It is simply an **extreme energetic imbalance**.
