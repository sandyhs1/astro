**Step 3: The Upapada Lagna (UL) \- The Real Marriage**

**Forget the 7th house for a second. The Upapada Lagna (the Arudha of the 12th house) tells us the *physical reality* of the marriage.**

* **If the 2nd house from UL has Rahu or Saturn, the marriage is "poisoned."**  
* **If the UL is in a 6/8/12 relationship with the Arudha Lagna (AL), the person the native *pretends* to be is at war with the person they are *married* to. This is where secret lives are born.**

**Step 4: The D60 (Shashtiamsa) \- The Final Word**

**For those who want "brutal" accuracy, the D60 is the court of final appeal.**
