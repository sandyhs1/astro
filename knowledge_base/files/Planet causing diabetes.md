# Vivah Saham Point: Predict Marriage, Love, Relationships in Vedic Astrology

The Vivah Saham Point is a sensitive point in your vedic astrology horoscope and is used to help **accurately** predict Marriage, Love, and Relationships. This point is calculated by adding the longitudes of Lagna Lord (LL) and 7th House Lord (7L).

A ***hidden secret in Vedic Astrology*** is when Jupiter is positioned or aspect the Vivah Saham Point; Marriage and Relationship are activated.

Calculation Formula:

The standard formula for Vivah Saham is:

* Formula:Venus Longitude \- Saturn Longitude \+Ascendant Longitude.  
* Adjustment: If the Ascendant does not fall between Venus and Saturn, 30°are added.  
* Night Birth: For night births, the formula is sometimes taken as Saturn Venus \+ Ascendant

**Usage in Astrology:**

* Timing Marriage: It indicates when planetary periods (dasha/bhukti) or transits act upon this specific point, creating the "marriage moment".  
* Transits: Benefic planets (like Jupiter or Venus) transiting over or aspecting the Vivah Saham sign suggest marriage prospects.  
* Varshaphala (Annual Chart): Vivah Saham or its lord will often establish a relationship with the 7th house or 7th lord in the annual chart during the year of marriage.

It is important to note that Vivah Saham is a distinct tool for timing and is often used alongside other techniques like Darakaraka and 7th house analysis.
