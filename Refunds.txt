Refund Policy — Quantum Karma

Last updated: 15 February 2026

We’ll be honest with you first:

This is not a downloadable movie.
Not a subscription you forgot to cancel.
Not a physical item you can return in a box.

Every report we create is made specifically for one person — you.

Once we begin work, time, analysis, and review are already invested.
Because of that, refunds are generally not offered.

We’d rather be clear upfront than argue later.

Why Refunds Are Not Encouraged

Your report is created using:

your birth details

manual verification

structured analysis

human interpretation

careful formatting and delivery

The moment we start preparing it, the work cannot be “un-done”.

So our default position is simple:

No refunds after purchase.

Not because we want to be rigid —
but because this is a personalized service, not a mass-produced product.

Rare Exceptions (Yes, They Exist — But Rare)

We may consider a refund only if:

the report is clearly unrelated to the details you provided

the report was not delivered at all

a verified technical failure prevented completion

In those cases, email us within 7 days at:

help@soulsync.tech

We will manually review it.

If we agree something genuinely went wrong on our side, we’ll fix it — and if fixing isn’t possible, we’ll refund.

What Is NOT a Valid Refund Reason

We cannot refund because:

you expected different answers

you disagree with interpretation

the content felt uncomfortable

your situation didn’t change

you changed your mind after purchase

someone else read it differently

life continued to be life

This product offers perspective, not guaranteed outcomes.

Before You Purchase
