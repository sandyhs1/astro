Privacy Policy — Quantum Karma

Last updated: 15 Feb 2026

We don’t like creepy apps.
So we built this to avoid becoming one.

This page explains what data we collect, why we collect it, what we do with it, and what we don’t do with it.

If you’re uncomfortable sharing personal birth details, you should not use this service. The product cannot exist without that information.

This service is operated from Bangalore, India.

1. What Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to generate your report and run the service properly.

Information you provide

Name (can be nickname)

Date of birth

Time of birth

Place of birth

Email address

Your stated concern (career, relationship, direction etc.)

Optional notes you write to us

Without birth details, the system cannot function. We do not ask for anything unrelated like contacts, photos, phone gallery, or social accounts.

Automatically collected information

Standard website data:

IP address

Device type

Browser type

Pages visited

Purchase status

This helps prevent fraud and fix bugs. Not to profile your personality.

Payment data

Payments are processed by third-party payment processors.
We do not store your card or UPI credentials on our servers.

We only receive confirmation that payment succeeded or failed.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information strictly to operate the service.

That includes:

generating your chart

structuring your report

reviewing it manually

delivering it to you

responding to support questions

improving clarity and accuracy

We do not use your birth details to advertise to you across the internet.
We do not sell your data to brokers.

3. About AI / LLM Usage (Important)

We use multiple language models (LLMs) as tools.

They help with:

structuring reports

formatting explanations

organizing insights

They do NOT replace human interpretation.

Every report is interpreted and reviewed by a human before delivery.

The models assist presentation.
They do not decide meaning.

Also — rare mistakes can happen in interpretation.
That’s unavoidable in any interpretive system, including astrology itself.

Which is why we provide post-delivery support and clarification.

We actively correct misunderstandings when users contact us.

If anyone claims astrology can be 100% accurate every time — you should question that claim. No human or system can guarantee certainty.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using access-restricted systems.

We apply:

encryption in transit

restricted access

minimal internal visibility

Only people directly involved in generating your report can access identifiable data.

We do not pass raw personal birth details around casually inside the company.

5. How Long We Keep Data

We keep identifiable data only as long as needed to:

deliver your report

provide support

handle disputes

After that:
We may retain anonymized pattern data (no names, no emails) to improve quality.

You may request deletion anytime by emailing help@soulsync.tech

We will remove identifiable personal data unless legally required to retain transaction records.

6. Third-Party Services

We rely on infrastructure providers for:

hosting

payment processing

email delivery

analytics

They process limited data required to perform their role.

They do not own your birth data and cannot legally reuse it for their purposes.

7. We Do Not Sell Personal Data

Let’s make this simple:

We do not sell, rent, trade, or monetize your birth details.

Our revenue comes from reports — not from your identity.

If that ever changes, this policy would be rewritten clearly.

8. Your Rights

You may:

request a copy of stored personal data

request correction

request deletion

withdraw consent for future use

Email: help@soulsync.tech

We respond manually — not automated bots.

9. Children’s Privacy

This service is not designed for children under 18.

We do not knowingly store children’s personal data independently of a parent or guardian.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this policy when the product evolves.

We won’t secretly expand data usage without updating this page.

Continued use after update means acceptance.

11. Final Clarity

We built Quantum Karma to give perspective — not surveillance.

Your personal information exists here only to generate your report and support you afterwards.

Nothing more.
